What is Web3?

Web3 is the next generation of the internet—decentralized, user-owned, and powered by blockchain technology. Unlike traditional Web2 platforms, Web3 offers transparency, privacy, and ownership of digital assets.

Core Web3 Technologies

  • Blockchain: The backbone of decentralization.
  • NFTs: Unique digital assets for art, identity, and ownership.
  • DeFi: Financial tools without banks or middlemen.
  • DAOs: Community-owned organizations.
  • Decentralized Domains: Own your digital identity (like 3.brave).
